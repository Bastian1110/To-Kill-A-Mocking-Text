The rapid development and increasing use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) have significantly impacted our daily routines. It's undeniable that AI has transformed educational methodologies, despite facing numerous obstacles and ethical dilemmas in its implementation across the educational landscape. This research aims to dissect the potential, advantages, and difficulties presented by AI within the educational sphere. A systematic literature review was conducted to pinpoint the prevailing topics of study and to furnish a comprehensive perspective on AI's role in education for both current educators and future scholarly exploration. The results indicate that the integration of AI in education is more prevalent in industrialized nations, particularly during the Industry 4.0 period. The paper also highlights various issues and provides pertinent recommendations.
