The domain of artificial intelligence (AI) is extensive, encompassing a variety of singular and combined AI-driven technologies. Among these, computer vision technology stands out. It is closely associated with machine learning (ML), deep learning (DL), and artificial neural networks among other technologies. Computer vision finds its utility in numerous sectors, with one of the most prominent recent applications being in the healthcare industry. Here, different algorithms from the mentioned technologies play a crucial role in extracting valuable information from medical imagery. This section discusses the fundamentals of computer vision, its various applications, and specifically its role in healthcare. Furthermore, the use of computer vision for tumor detection in a MATLAB setting is examined.
